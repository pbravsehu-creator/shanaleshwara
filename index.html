The Swayambhu Lingam

Inside is the massive Swayambhu (Self-Manifested) Lingam of Lord Shiva. It embodies the inherent power of Shiva and requires no 'Prana Pratishtha'.

Divine History

Built in 1592 by the Maharaja of Patiala. Since the 15th century, this temple has been a center for Sadhus and the Guru-Shishya tradition.
