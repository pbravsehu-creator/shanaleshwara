Divine History

Situated in the serene Nalas village, the Shanaleshwara Swayambhu Temple is home to a 600-year-old self-manifested (Swayambhu) Shivling. It does not require Prana Pratishtha as it inherently embodies Shiva's power.

The Legend: It is believed that a cow used to naturally shower her milk on this specific spot in the jungle, leading to the discovery of the divine Lingam by local cowherds.

The current structure was built in 1592 by the Maharaja of Patiala and follows the Guru-Shishya tradition.
