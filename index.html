It is situated 7 Kilometres away in the Nalas village of Rajpura, Punjab. It is maintained by the ascetic sadhus of the Juna Akhara Foundation.

The Divine Swayambhu

Inside the Shanaleshwara Temple is the Swayambhu lingam of Lord Shiva. Shiva in the lingam form is believed to be Swayambhu.

Swayambhu lingams are self-created or natural lingams, discovered in the place where they now stand. Most of these are oval-shaped stones.

Importance & Festival

Maha Shivaratri is a great festival here; a three-day fair is held every year and lakhs of people come to ask for a vow.
