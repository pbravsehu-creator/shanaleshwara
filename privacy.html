Security & Privacy Policy

Effective Date: February 2026

1. Information We Collect

The Shanaleshwara Swayambhu Temple website is a 100% non-profit digital sewa initiative. We respect your privacy. We do not require you to create an account, and we do not collect sensitive personal data directly through this website.

2. Third-Party Links & Services

Our website utilizes third-party services such as Google Maps for navigation and WhatsApp for our Digital Sangat. When you click these external links, you are subject to the respective privacy and security policies of Google and WhatsApp.

3. Data Security

We prioritize your digital safety. We apply standard web security practices to ensure your browsing experience is secure. As a matter of personal security, we encourage all devotees to follow the principle of "Never Trust, Always Verify" and ensure they are visiting the official domain.

4. Changes to This Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy periodically to reflect any changes in our digital services. All updates will be posted on this page.
