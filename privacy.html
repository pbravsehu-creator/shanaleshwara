Security & Privacy Policy

Effective Date: February 2026

Zero-Data Initiative: Shanaleshwara.vercel.app is a static informational website. We do not store, process, or sell any user data. Your presence here is completely anonymous.

Our website is hosted on the Vercel platform as a static project. We do not have a backend database or registration forms. This means we do not collect names, email addresses, or phone numbers from our visitors.

2. Hosting & Infrastructure

Since this site is hosted via Vercel, it benefits from Vercel's global edge network security. 3. Third-Party Interactions

We provide links to Google Maps (for directions) and WhatsApp (for the Digital Sangat group). Once you leave our domain, your data is governed by the privacy policies of those respective platforms. We do not receive any personal data from these services.

4. SSL Security

Your connection to this website is encrypted using SSL (HTTPS) provided by Vercel. This ensures that your browsing journey to the Shanaleshwara Temple remains private and secure from interception.
